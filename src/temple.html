New Arrival

Chronograph 5172G

The new chronograph features a white gold case with round guilloched pushers and three-tier lugs. Its vintage aesthetic is underscored by a sapphire-crystal "box" glass. The dial features charcoal gray gold applied numerals with luminescent coating.
